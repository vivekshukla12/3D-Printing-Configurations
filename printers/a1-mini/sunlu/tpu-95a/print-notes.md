# Print Notes

- Dry at 55°C before every critical print.
- Dry a new spool for 4–6 hours.
- Dry a wet spool for 6–8 hours.
- Print directly from the filament dryer whenever possible.
- Increase the nozzle temperature to 230°C if under-extrusion occurs.
- Reduce the nozzle temperature to 220°C if excessive stringing occurs.
- Tune further only after checking dimensional accuracy, extrusion consistency, and stringing performance.
